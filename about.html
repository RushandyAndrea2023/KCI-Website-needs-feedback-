About us

Overview

Kentec Communications, Inc. (KCI) is an Internet provider, based in Sterling, Colorado. KCI is the only provider of Fiber To The Home in Sterling, offering broadband internet - currently the fastest residential broadband internet service available in NorthEast Colorado. KCI also maintains an extensive wireless Internet network throughout NorthEastern Colorado and into the Nebraska panhandle. KCI additionally offers web and email hosting.
